Rules

  • Each player submits the name of a famous person or fictional character.

  • Once All players have submitted their name, players can look at the list for 10 seconds and then may not look at the list again

  • The first player then begins by addressing any one other player and says are you ___ ?

  • If they are correct, the other player joins the first player's team and they question another person.

  • If they are incorrect, the chance to ask a question moves to the player to whom the question was posed, and they ask another player a question.

  • The game continues until everyone is one one big team.
